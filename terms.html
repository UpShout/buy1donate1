Section 12 — Our Core Commitment

Charitable Giving Commitment

As part of our buy1donate1 mission, Upshout LLC commits to the following charitable giving practice in connection with t-shirt sales made through any sales channel operated or authorised by us.

Donation Calculation

At the end of each calendar month, we will tally the total number of t-shirts sold during that month across all applicable sales channels. For each t-shirt sold, we will make one charitable donation as described below.

Form of Donation

For each t-shirt sold, we will donate either (a) one plain t-shirt of equivalent or similar quality, or (b) a monetary amount equal to fifty percent (50%) of the net margin on that t-shirt, calculated as the sale price minus our cost of goods. The choice of donation form — in-kind or monetary — is made at our sole discretion based on operational and logistical factors.

Recipient Selection

We do not maintain standing partnerships with any charitable organisation. Each month, we identify and contact a charitable organisation of our choosing to receive that month's donation. Recipients may vary month to month. We select organisations that serve individuals in need of clothing or other basic necessities, but we make no guarantee that the same organisation will be selected in any two consecutive months.

No Consumer Obligation or Surcharge

The charitable donation is funded entirely by Upshout LLC from our proceeds. Customers are not charged any additional fee in connection with this programme, and no portion of any customer's purchase price is directly transferred to a charity.

Limitations

We reserve the right to modify or discontinue this programme at any time, provided that any such change will not affect donations owed for sales already completed in a prior calendar month. We make no representation that this programme qualifies for any particular tax treatment by customers.
